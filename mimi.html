About

I am a researcher, editor and writer from Toronto with an interest in food, travel, culture, lifestyle, business and technology. Currently, I am a librarian in the film group at the Ontario Media Development Corporation. Prior to that, I worked in magazine and online publishing, starting as a fact checker for Toronto Life magazine and later becoming the online listings editor of torontolife.com and where.ca. I hold a Bachelor of Journalism degree in Magazine Journalism from Ryerson University and a Master of Information Studies in Library and Information Science from University of Toronto. My writing has appeared in newspapers, magazines and websites including BlogTO, Techvibes, The Scarborough Mirror, Shameless magazine and Masthead.

Film Locations Database

The Digitial Location Library assists producers in the film and TV industry in findiing places in Ontario to shoot movies, TV shows, commercials, shorts or more. Functioning as both a photo collection and research tool for space rentals, this database features more than 9000 listings and 250,000 photos of film-friendly locations.
