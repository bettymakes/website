About

I am a researcher from Toronto with an interest in food, travel, art, culture, business, technology and city life. Currently, I am a librarian in the film group at the Ontario Media Development Corporation. Prior to that, I had worked in magazine and web publishing, starting as an intern and fact checker at Toronto Life magazine, and later becoming the online listings editor of torontolife.com and where.ca. In addition to experience managing research and development projects as well as digital services, I occasionally write for publications and websites. My pieces have appeared on BlogTO, Techvibes, The Scarborough Mirror and Shameless magazine. I hold a Bachelor of Journalism degree in Magazine Journalism from Ryerson University and a Master of Information Studies in Library and Information Science from University of Toronto.

Film Locations Database

The Digitial Location Library assists producers in the film and TV industry in findiing places in Ontario to shoot movies, TV shows, documentaries, commercials, shorts and more. Functioning as both a photo collection and research tool for space rentals, this database features more than 9000 listings and 250,000 images of locations.
