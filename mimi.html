About

I am a content manager, researcher, editor and terrible troubleshooter from Toronto. I started my career in magazine and web publishing, first as an intern and fact checker at Toronto Life magazine, and later became the online listings editor of torontolife.com and where.ca. Currently, I am a librarian in the film group at the Ontario Media Development Corporation. I hold a Bachelor of Journalism degree in Magazine Journalism from Ryerson University and a Master of Information Studies in Library and Information Science from University of Toronto.

Portfolio

Here are a couple of things I have worked on:

Film Locations Database

The Digitial Location Library is a database containing more than 9000 listings and 250,000 photos of locations in Ontario to assist film and TV producers in finding places to shoot their project. As a key research tool during the pre-production stage of filming, this service helps industry professionals select the perfect real-world setting to match a scene in a movie, TV show, documentary, commerical or short.

Online Listings

Torontolife.com's restaurant, bars & clubs and food shop listings and reviews highlight more than 1400 local hotspots for drinking and dining. I managed the content on this part of the website, which was an online version of Toronto Life magazine's Eating & Drinking Guide. I also worked with editors, fact checkers and developers on a digital project to redesign and relaunch three databases.
