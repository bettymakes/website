About

I am a researcher, editor and writer from Toronto with an interest in food, travel, culture, lifestyle, business and technology. I began my career as a fact checker for Toronto Life magazine and later became the online listings editor of torontolife.com and where.ca. Currently, I am a librarian in the film group at the Ontario Media Development Corporation. My writing has appeared on where.ca, torontolife.com, BlogTO, Techvibes, The Scarborough Mirror and more. For formal reasons, I hold a Bachelor of Journalism degree in Magazine Journalism from Ryerson University and a Master of Information Studies in Library and Information Science from University of Toronto.
